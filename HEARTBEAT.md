# HEARTBEAT.md

Check a few times per day, but stay quiet unless something concrete matters.

## Good reasons to reach out
- a real reminder tied to a known commitment
- an upcoming calendar event
- an important message or email
- a useful proactive suggestion with clear value

## Good reasons to stay quiet
- nothing changed
- it would just be generic nagging
- it is late and not urgent
- the human is clearly busy

## Rule

If nothing meaningful changed, do not manufacture a reason to speak.
